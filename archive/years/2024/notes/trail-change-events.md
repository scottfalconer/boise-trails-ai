Source: user-provided excerpts from past Boise Trails Challenge emails, recorded on 2026-05-04.

## Events

- 2024-06-18: Heroes Trail parking/construction access issue. The hospital parking lot at the south end of Heroes Trail was being redone until June 22; participants could wait or do an out-and-back from Elephant Rock.
- 2024-06-18: Bogus Basin XC trail construction. The organizer note described significant XC trail changes and Lower Deer Point closure, making Around the Mountain a longer trek.
- 2024-06-24: Polecat Gulch fire closure. The organizer note said Polecat was closed by fire and was temporarily marked complete for athletes who had completed everything else while organizers waited to see whether it would reopen.
