# USFS Shingle Trailhead Probe

Objective: test whether the USFS Shingle Creek Trailhead improves the strict
p90 blockers around Shingle Creek, Dry Creek, and Sweet Connie.

Source: [USFS Shingle Creek Trailhead](https://www.fs.usda.gov/r04/boise/recreation/shingle-creek-trailhead)

## Result

The probe is graph-validated and track-valid, but it is not a solution for the
strict 260-minute p90 proof.

| Segment | Trail | P90 | P75 | On Foot | Track |
|---:|---|---:|---:|---:|---|
| 1656 | Shingle Creek Trail | 428 | 382 | 18.06 mi | true |
| 1545 | Dry Creek Trail | 314 | 280 | 13.33 mi | true |
| 1667 | Sweet Connie Trail | 461 | 411 | 19.78 mi | true |

## Verdict

Do not promote this anchor as the p90 proof solution for the current blockers.
It may still be useful as a public access point for other route designs, but the
tested single-segment loops are too long for the current p90 bound.
