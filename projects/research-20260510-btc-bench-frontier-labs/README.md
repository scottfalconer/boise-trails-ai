## Research Question

Can the Boise Trails AI project credibly support a research bundle for:

> BTC-Bench: Evaluating LLM agents on field-executable spatial planning under real-world constraints.

## Short Answer

Yes. The repo supports a focused benchmark/evaluation framing. The strongest claim is not route optimization novelty. It is a verifier-backed agent benchmark where success requires maintaining truth across official segment rules, spatial geometry, generated artifacts, field cues, access/logistics constraints, actual field feedback, and recertification after state changes.
